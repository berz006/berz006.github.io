 Welcome 

In Colonial Williamsburg, a checkerboard signpost outside of an establishment symbolized the entertainment and joy to be found inside. Likewise, we welcome you to experience the entertainment and joy to be found in our services, whether you are seeking finished goods, patterns for projects, or instruction in decorative painting. Our investment of time, talent, and tenderness results in an entertaining tole-painted treasure which brings timeless delight and gratification.
