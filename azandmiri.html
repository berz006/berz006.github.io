Az and Miri

Nearly sixteen years ago a pregnant kitty wandered into our lives. Five days later she gave us six grand-kitties, five of them calico girls, born on my Gram's birthday. It was a sign.........Two of the kitties were adopted by my sons; and wouldn't you know it? They were my favorites! But I never encouraged them to choose these two. Jeff chose Azrael, who was his partner in crime in many things, even moving cross-country to Colorado. She was a lover and very vocal and talked with me on the phone many times. She was especially colorful. We lost her in 2015. Steve took Miriamele, named after a character in a book he had read. Not as colorful or vocal, but just as sweet and loving. We lost her in 2016. This design is a tribute to them and the loving sons who cared for them.
