Ingredients:

  • 1/2 lb. butter
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 6 eggs
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 t. baking soda
  • 1 t. vanilla
  • 1/2 t. lemon extract
  • 1/4 t. almond extract
  • 1 t. orange extract
  • 1/2 t. rum extract
  • 1/2 cup apricot brandy
  1. Cream butter; add sugar
  2. Add eggs, continue beating
  3. Add baking soda, then half flour followed by half sour cream
  4. Beat until smooth
  5. Repeat
  6. Add extracts and then brandy
    1. Pour into two greased loaf pans
    2. Bake at 350° for an hour and fifteen minutes (watch)
  8. Or
    1. Pour into six small greased loaf pans
    2. Bake at 350° for forty-five to fifty minutes
